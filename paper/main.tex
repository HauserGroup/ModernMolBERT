\begin{abstract}
We introduce \textsc{ModernMolBERT}, a Modern Molecular BERT family for small-molecule representation learning. \textsc{ModernMolBERT} adapts the ModernBERT encoder architecture to SELFIES molecular language modeling, using chemically aware tokenization to preserve molecular syntax and structure. We train base and large models on large-scale unlabeled molecular corpora and evaluate the resulting representations on standard molecular property prediction benchmarks. \textsc{ModernMolBERT} provides a compact, reproducible, encoder-only foundation for SELFIES-based chemical machine learning.
\end{abstract}
